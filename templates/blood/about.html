About Us

Blood bank is a place where blood bag that is collected from blood donation event is stored.
The word "Blood Bank" refers to a division of a hospital laboratary where the storage of
blood product occurs and where proper testing is performed to reduce the risk of tranfusion 
related events.
The process of managing the blood bag that is received from blood donation events need a 
proper and systematic management. 
Blood Bank donation system can collect blood from many donators in short from various sources 
and distribute the blood to needy people who require blood. Even though the technology has
advanced much already ,still today's system in blood bank are usually manual system. Usually 
when a person required a particular type of blood and the type is not available inthe hospital,
the person send ,messages to family,relatives and friends if they can donate. As such, it is 
time consuming and laborious and may risk the life of the person who really ned blood urgently.

The project's aim is to develop an application system to minimize the manual work for Blood 
Bank,Donors, Blood Group management.It monitors all of the Blood Group information,Blood cells,
Blood supply and Donor list.The development of Blood Bank and Donation Management System is 
proposed to provide a management functional to the blood bank in order to handle the blood
bag and to make entriesof the individuals who want to donate blood and who are in need.


Advantages:
 
1.Helps Blood Banks to automate blood doner and depository online.
2.Encourages blood donors to donate.
3.Helps people find blood donors in times of need.
